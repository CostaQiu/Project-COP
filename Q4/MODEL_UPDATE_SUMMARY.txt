MODEL EQUATION (SIMPLIFIED)
================================================================================

Sales_t = β0 + β1*Sales_t-1 + β2*Sales_t-4 + β3*Oil_Q_avg_t + ε_t

Where:
  - β0: Intercept (constant term)
  - β1: Sales autoregressive coefficient (~0.67)
  - β2: Year-over-year sales effect (~-0.17)
  - β3: Oil price impact (~92 per $1)
  - ε_t: Residual error

Interpretation:
  - 67% of previous quarter sales carries forward
  - YoY effect is slightly negative (-17%)
  - Each $1/barrel increase in oil →  ~$92k sales increase
  - No strong seasonal effects detected (Q2-Q4 dummies removed)
  - Profitability 1 year ago doesn't predict current sales (ROIC removed)
